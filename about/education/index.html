Professional Writing B.A.

I am currently in my second semester at Michigan State University. I am taking a variety of courses across multiple disciplines. Currently, I am practicing the principles of design, editing, and communication. I am learning what motivates an individual’s mind and how they interact with others. I am analyzing the aspects of rhetoric that motivate and inspire. I am taking down a language barrier one grammatical element at a time.

My Professional Writing major combines my love of writing with my love of people. It’s a program that enriches my knowledge and skills, and it’s a community that allows me to thrive.
