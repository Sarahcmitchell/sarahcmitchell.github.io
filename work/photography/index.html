Photography

I use photography to capture the beautiful, funny, and interesting moments in life. Although I have experience using DSLR cameras, I don’t own one, so I mostly use my iPhone 5S camera. The precision and quality of a DSLR is lost, but I’ve found that a phone camera allows for flexibility and spontaneity that just isn’t possible with larger cameras. These photos have been edited in Adobe Photoshop.
